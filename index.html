About Us

Meet our company

Aeronautics and Maritime Safety Institute (AMSI) trains civil and military personnel in charge of safety and security in all transport and Land Transport sectors. Training covers familiarization, prevention, safety management, human factors and accident investigations. All courses are constantly updated and take into account the latest advances in best practices regarding Risk and Safety Management, the key parts of safety. We also deliver custom Consulting taking into consideration today’s security concerns on the ground, sea, and in the air, changes in systems and regulations, latest maintenance and flight/marine technologies and recent accident statistics.
